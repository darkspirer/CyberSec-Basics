# Data maintenance 
- what is encryption  -  Encryption is the process of converting information into a form in which unauthorized parties cannot read it
# How do you encrypt your data ?
Software programs are used to encrypt files, folders and even entire drives 
- Step
  - 1 step - Select one or more files or folders.
  - 2 step - Right click the selected data and go to ‘Properties.’
  - 3 step - Find and click ‘Advanced.’
  - 4 step - Select the ‘Encrypt contents to secure data’ check box.
  - 5 step - Files and folders that have been encrypted with EFS are displayed in green as shown here.
