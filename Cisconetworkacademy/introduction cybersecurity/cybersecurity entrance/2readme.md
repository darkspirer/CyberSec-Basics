#  the cube 
the McCumber Cube is a model framework created by John McCumber in 1991 to help organizations establish and evaluate information security initiatives by considering all of the related factors that impact them.
- the foundational principles for protecting information
  - confidentiality
  - integrity
  - availability
- the protection of informationin each state
  - processing
  - storage
  - transmission
- the security measures used to protect data
  - awareness training and education
  - technology
  - policy and procedure
- Data theft mostly happens through phishing. In this method, our information is stolen by deceiving us
